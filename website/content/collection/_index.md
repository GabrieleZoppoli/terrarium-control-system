The full collection spans 356 plant acquisitions across 81 genera, of which approximately 115 currently live in the highland terrarium and the rest occupy outdoor beds, windowsills, and smaller terraria. Three years of cultivation data: 67% overall survival, 100% in *Heliamphora*, highland *Nepenthes*, and *Dracula*.
