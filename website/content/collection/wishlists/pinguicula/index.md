Seven Mexican butterworts across two vendors, chosen for what the current collection lacks: yellow flowers, dark violet flowers, veined violets, and three morphological oddities. Prioritized by flower color uniqueness over species completionism.

## Context

The picks fill color gaps (yellow, dark violet, veined violet) and morphological gaps (giant flowers, tentacle rosette, alien rosette) absent from the current collection. BCP (bestcarnivorousplants.net) may reopen May 2026 — worth checking for *elizabethiae*, *pilosa*, *robertiana*, *nivalis*, and *zamudioana*, none of which are available elsewhere in Europe.
