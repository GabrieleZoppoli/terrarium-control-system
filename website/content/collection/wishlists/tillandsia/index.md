Two lists, one urgent. The Hromadnik order is the priority — Lieselotte Hromadnik is elderly, ships from her home in Kritzendorf, and carries type lineages and namesake species that nobody else does. PlantaBrutt is commercial and will be around longer, so its list is where oddball size/form variants live.

### Considered, not in this cart

- *T. ehlersiana* (€15) — fleshy silver bulb, turns pink under stress
- *T. latifolia* (€8) — hugest grey species, rosettes 60+ cm
- *T. caulescens* (€8) — tallest grey species, 1 m+ caulescent stems
- *T. mima* (€20) — ghost-white funnel rosette
- *T. malyi* (€20) — rare *tectorum* relative
- *T. argentina* (€7) — cutest tiny silver egg
- *T. tectorum* f. *gigantea* (up to €20) — giant Ecuadorian *tectorum* form
