## Cloud Forest Terrarium

The terrarium houses approximately 115 living species from 42 genera, sourced from cloud forests and highland habitats worldwide. Despite originating from different continents and evolutionary lineages, these plants thrive under the same conditions because their native habitats share functionally identical climates: cool temperatures, persistent fog, and high humidity.

Over 3+ years of cultivation, 171 plants have been trialled with an overall survival rate of 67% (115 alive, 49 lost, 7 traded/given away). The highest survival rates are seen in *Heliamphora* (100%), highland *Nepenthes* (100%), and *Dracula* (100%). Losses are concentrated among species requiring dry rest periods or warmer conditions.
