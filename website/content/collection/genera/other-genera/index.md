## Utricularia sect. Orchidioides (Terrarium)

Epiphytic bladderworts from tepui cliff faces and Neotropical cloud forest canopies, cultivated kokedama-style in the terrarium's middle zone. Only the epiphytic species sit in the terrarium; terrestrial *Utricularia* are grown elsewhere.
