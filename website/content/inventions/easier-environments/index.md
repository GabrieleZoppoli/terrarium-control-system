Not every plant needs a 1.5 m climate-controlled cabinet. Several species in the collection live in simpler containers tuned to require minimal daily intervention.

## Setups

*To be documented. Likely candidates include:*
- Sealed jars with sphagnum and small carnivores or epiphytes
- Outdoor *Sarracenia* / *Dionaea* / temperate *Drosera* bog tray
- Windowsill *Tillandsia* and other dry-tolerant epiphytes
- Unheated cool-grow boxes for hardy orchids

Each setup will get its own subsection with: target species, substrate, watering interval, light source, observed survival.
