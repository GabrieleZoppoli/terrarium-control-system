Three projects from one grower in Genoa, Italy.

## [Highland Terrarium](highland/)

A 1.5 × 0.6 × 1.1 m insulated cabinet that simulates highland cloud forest weather using real-time Colombian meteorological data (time-shifted 15 hours). Marine refrigeration drives nighttime to ~13.5 °C in a room at 22 °C. ~120 species coexist under one regime. Open-source control system; multi-paper publication in progress.

## [Inventions](inventions/)

Smaller plant-keeping experiments: a self-watering Zeer pot for *Darlingtonia*, a dedicated *Drosera regia* setup, and lower-effort enclosures for less demanding species.

## [Collection](collection/)

356 plants across 81 genera — browsed by phylogenetic dendrogram or by genus. Three years of cultivation data, 67% overall survival, with detailed per-genus pages for sources, prices, and observed performance.
